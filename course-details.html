Certified Public Accountant (CPA)
Overview

The Certified Public Accountant (CPA) course is a professional qualification offered by KASNEB. It equips learners with skills in accounting, auditing, finance, taxation, and corporate governance.

Structure of the CPA Course

The CPA course consists of three levels, each building on the previous one:

Level 1: Foundation Level
  • Financial Accounting
  • Communication Skills
  • Principles of Management
  • Business and Corporate Law
Level 2: Intermediate Level
  • Company Law
  • Financial Management
  • Taxation
  • Auditing and Assurance
Level 3: Advanced Level
  • Advanced Financial Reporting
  • Strategic Management and Leadership
  • Advanced Auditing and Assurance
  • Public Finance and Taxation
Entry Requirements
  • KCSE Aggregate C+ (with C+ in Mathematics & English/Kiswahili)
  • Accounting Technician Diploma (ATD) from KASNEB
  • Degree from a recognized university
  • Equivalent qualification approved by KASNEB
Examinations & Certification

Exams are conducted twice a year (May & November). After passing all levels, students can register as CPA-K with the Institute of Certified Public Accountants of Kenya (ICPAK).

  • Accountant â€“ Financial reporting & bookkeeping
  • Auditor â€“ Internal & external auditing
  • Tax Consultant â€“ Tax advisory & compliance
  • Financial Analyst â€“ Investment analysis & risk management
  • Chief Financial Officer (CFO) â€“ Corporate financial leadership
  • University Lecturer/Trainer â€“ Teaching accounting & finance

  • Globally recognized professional qualification

  • Pathway to advanced studies (ACCA, CFA, MBA)
  • Opportunities for self-employment as a consultant

Certified Secretaries (CS)
Overview

The Certified Secretaries (CS) qualification is a professional course offered by KASNEB. It equips learners with expertise in governance, corporate law, compliance, administration, and management, preparing them for leadership roles in corporate governance and regulatory compliance.

The CS course consists of three levels, progressively building knowledge in governance, law, and secretarial duties.

Level 1: Foundation Level
  • Foundations of Business Law
  • Principles of Management
  • Business Communication
  • Introduction to Financial Accounting
Level 2: Intermediate Level
  • Corporate Governance and Ethics
  • Company Law
  • Financial Management
  • Human Resource Management

Entry Requirements

  • Diploma in a related field or other qualification approved by KASNEB
Examinations & Certification

Exams are conducted twice a year (May & November). After completing all levels, students are awarded the Certified Secretaries (CS) qualification by KASNEB.

Credit Management (CAMS)
Overview

The Credit Management (CAMS) course is a foundation-level qualification offered by KASNEB. It equips learners with essential skills in credit management, debt recovery, customer service, and financial operations. CAMS graduates can work in credit departments, financial institutions, and debt collection agencies.

Structure of the CAMS Course

The CAMS course consists of two levels, designed to provide practical knowledge in credit risk assessment and debt management.

Level 1
  • Principles of Credit Management
  • Business Mathematics
  • Foundations of Accounting
  • Business Communication
Level 2
  • Credit Risk Management
  • Legal Aspects of Credit Management
  • Debt Collection and Recovery
  • Customer Relationship Management
Entry Requirements
  • KCSE Aggregate D (or any other equivalent qualification approved by KASNEB)
Examinations & Certification

Exams are conducted twice a year (May & November). Upon successful completion, students are awarded the Credit Management (CAMS) certificate by KASNEB.
